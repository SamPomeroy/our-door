Our Door

Live demo story by Three's Company

Problem

Problem + User

Getting stuck is not just about the answer.

Students in technical learning programs get stuck all the time, but the problem is not just not knowing the answer. It is not knowing where to go next.

Student Needs private guided help in the moment.
Curriculum Can feel messy or hard to navigate when pressure is high.
Instructor Needs visibility into where students are getting stuck.

Solution + Why AI

A curriculum-aware Socratic assistant.

Our Door guides students using a three-knocks model instead of giving direct answers.

Hint A small nudge toward the relevant concept.
Curriculum reference A pointer back to course material or a learned pattern.
Next step A concrete action the student can take right now.

AI fits because it can retrieve curriculum context and generate guided responses in real time, while guardrails keep responses aligned with the learning philosophy.

Impact

A doorway into understanding.

Our Door is not just a chatbot. Students get private guided support without simply being given answers, and instructors gain visibility into confusion patterns they may otherwise miss.

Student impact A safer place to ask and a clearer next step.
Instructor impact Earlier visibility into repeated confusion.
Learning impact The goal is not just getting unstuck. It is learning better.
